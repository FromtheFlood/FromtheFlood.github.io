Key lime pie

Pictured above is a deconstrusted key lime pie. This dessert expertly balances the bitterness of limes, using sugars, lemons, and a hibiscus syrup to create a rounded flavour profile perfect to end an evening on a high note

A tip for creating a less bitter yet still flavorful pie is to slightly age freshly squeezed lime juice. 4 hours is about the sweet spot, with anything over 10 making the dish less flavourful and often allowing other flavours to overpower the limes
